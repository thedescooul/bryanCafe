Privacy Policy

How Bryan's Café collects and protects customer information.

1. Information We Collect

Bryan's Café collects customer information only when a customer submits the enquiry form. This may include first name, last name, email address, message subject and message body.

2. How We Use Information

The information is used only to respond to customer enquiries and improve customer service. Bryan's Café does not sell or share customer information with third parties unless required by law.

3. Data Protection

Reasonable security procedures are followed to protect user information, including input validation, limited data collection and safe handling of customer details.

4. Customer Contact

Customers may contact Bryan's Café to ask questions about how their information is collected, used and handled.
